Frequently asked questions

Is MB MEDIA free to use? ▾
Yes. There's no sign-up and no hidden paywall on downloads. If you'd like to support ongoing hosting costs, there's an optional donate page.
Which platforms are supported? ▾
YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X (Twitter), Facebook, Vimeo, and Dailymotion. Support depends on the source platform's public availability at any given time.
Is downloading copyrighted content legal? ▾
Copyright law and each platform's terms of service still apply to whatever you download. Only download content you own, have permission to use, or that's clearly licensed for reuse. See our DMCA Policy for takedown requests.
Why did my download fail? ▾
Some content is geo-restricted, private, or age-gated by the source platform and can't be fetched. If a link consistently fails, try a different quality option or confirm the video is still public.
Does this work on mobile? ▾
Yes — the site is fully responsive. Clipboard auto-detect depends on your mobile browser's clipboard permissions.
Which browsers are supported? ▾
Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari, and Opera on both desktop and mobile.
