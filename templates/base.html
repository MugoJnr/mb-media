Cookies (optional)

Most people can skip this. Normal public downloads do not need your cookies. Upload only if a video fails because it is private, age-gated, or the site keeps blocking the request.

How to export cookies.txt

  1. Install a browser extension that exports Netscape cookies.txt (Chrome/Edge: “Get cookies.txt LOCALLY”; Firefox: “cookies.txt”).
  2. Open the site in your browser and log in (YouTube, Instagram, etc.).
  3. On that site’s tab, use the extension → Export / Download as cookies.txt.
  4. Upload that file below. We use it only for your browser session and auto-delete it after 6 hours.

Never share your cookies file — it can give access to your logged-in account.
