About MB MEDIA

Download Smarter. Faster. Better.

MB MEDIA is a media downloading tool built to strip away the clutter that's crept into most download sites — the redirect chains, the fake "download" buttons, the forced sign-ups. Paste a link, see exactly what you're getting, choose your format, and download it.

Who built it

MB MEDIA is created and maintained by MugoByte Technologies (MBT) — the same studio behind ExamHub Kenya. Built in Nairobi for everyday tools that stay clean and useful.

Our approach

No dark patterns, no forced ads before a download link appears, and no data sold to third parties. The tool exists to do one job well.
